Chicken Wrap

Description

Thereâ€™s nothing I love more than a quick lunch idea and chicken wraps are among my favorite. There is so much you can do with leftover chicken wrapped inside a tortilla or inside lettuce

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken tenders or other leftover chicken
  • 1/2 cup Buffalo sauce
  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1/4 cup Chunky Blue Cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded lettuce
    • Optional
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Tomatoes diced

Steps

  1. Chop up chicken tenders into small 1-inch or smaller pieces and place in a bowl.
  2. Pour Buffalo Sauce over chicken and mix to coat.
  3. Put 1/4 of the buffalo chicken, blue cheese, and lettuce into each wrap.
  4. Wrap each tortilla and secure with a toothpick if desired.
  5. Enjoy immediately or store up to 3 days in the refrigerator and enjoy cold or reheated.
